About Me:

Meng Xiao received his PhD in June 2023 and is currently a Special Research Assistant (postdoctoral fellow) working in the Computer Network Information Center, Chinese Academy of Sciences, mentored by Prof. Yuanchun Zhou. He has published several papers prolifically on top-tier venues such as IEEE TKDE, ACM TKDD, AIJ, NeurIPS, IJCAI, IEEE ICDM, etc. He also serve as a PC member or reviewer on many premier international conferences and journals. Meng Xiao is honored to have been advised by Prof. Yuanchun Zhou, Prof. Yanjie Fu, and Dr. Min Wu, and is delighted to collaborate with young scholars such as Prof. Ziyue Qiao and Prof. Dongjie Wang.

He has been honored with the Special Prize of the Chinese Academy of Sciences President Scholarship in 2023, and the Young Elite Scientists Sponsorship Program (2024-2026) by BAST.

Research Interests: Data-centric AI, AI4LifeScience, and Scientific Data Mining.
